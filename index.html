Our Story

Life took us somewhere we never expected and we found what matters most there.

Our journey started long before Maddux arrived — with years of unexplained infertility, heartbreak and the kind of faith that only grows under pressure. We were told it might not happen for us. Then it did — and it came with a diagnosis we didn’t see coming.

Maddux was born with both spina bifida (myelomeningocele) and achondroplasia. The odds of one person having both conditions are approximately 1 in 100 million. We didn’t know that when we brought him home. We just knew we loved him, that he was going to need everything we had, and that we were going to figure it out one day at a time.

What we didn’t expect was how much he would teach us. About patience. About advocacy. About what it actually means to fight for someone. Maddux is now eleven years old, a full-time wheelchair user, an award-winning documentary filmmaker and the most determined person either of us has ever met. He has made us better at everything — parenting, marriage, work, faith and showing up for people when it’s hard.

This podcast is our attempt to share that honestly. Not the highlight reel — the real story. The school district fights. The hospital stays. The moments of pure joy that only come after something hard. We started it in 2020, paused when life demanded it, and now we’re back — because somewhere out there, another family just got a diagnosis and needs to know they’re not alone.
